# Only customize the role description and verdict values.

You are a senior [NICHE] analyst. You previously scored this item independently. Now review your analysis alongside three other AI models and produce your final, refined verdict.

=== YOUR ORIGINAL ANALYSIS ===
{own_analysis}

=== OTHER MODELS' ANALYSES ===
{other_analyses}

=== DELIBERATION INSTRUCTIONS ===

**Step 1 — Compare scores across all 4 models**
For each scoring dimension, note the range across all models.

**Step 2 — Identify agreements**
Where do 3+ models agree within 3 points? These are likely well-calibrated and should not shift without new evidence.

**Step 3 — Investigate disagreements**
Where do scores diverge by more than 5 points, dig into why:
- Did another model cite specific evidence you did not account for?
- Did another model identify an alternative that changes the value score?
- Did another model flag a concern from the research data that you missed?

**Step 4 — Update your scores if warranted**
Revise a score ONLY if another model identified concrete evidence you missed.
Do NOT revise simply because others scored differently. If your reasoning is evidence-based and sound, hold your position.

**Step 5 — Hallucination check**
If any model cites a specific claim that you cannot verify from the research data or your knowledge — flag it. Do not incorporate unverifiable claims into your revised scoring.

**Step 6 — Finalize your verdict**
Produce your best final assessment.

=== HARD CONSTRAINTS ===
- base_score = exact sum of all component scores
- base_score must be between 0 and 100
- tier must match base_score: S+ (90–100), S (80–89), A (65–79), B (50–64), C (below 50)
- All component scores must be within their defined ranges
- Reasoning must be minimum 250 words and cite specific evidence

=== OUTPUT FORMAT ===

Return ONLY valid JSON — no markdown, no code fences, no preamble, no trailing text:
{{"llm": "{llmName}", "stage": 2, "product": "{productName}", "brand": "{brand}", "scores": {{"dimension_1": <0-WEIGHT>, "dimension_2": <0-WEIGHT>}}, "base_score": <exact sum of all scores>, "tier": "<S+|S|A|B|C>", "consumer_verdict": "<POSITIVE|NEUTRAL|NEGATIVE>", "reasoning": "<refined analysis, minimum 250 words, cite specific evidence>", "consensus_notes": "<what models agreed/disagreed on and how resolved>", "dimension_rulings": {{"dimension_1": "<range + ruling>", "dimension_2": "<range + ruling>"}}, "key_findings": ["<finding 1>", "<finding 2>", "<finding 3>"], "red_flags": ["<concern or empty array>"], "hallucinations_flagged": ["<flagged claim or empty array>"], "confidence": "<high|medium|low>", "confidence_reason": "<why>", "best_dupe": "<Alternative by Brand — $XX or null>", "verdict": "<one direct sentence>"}}
